Our Motto

Sai Baba Charitable Trust is an India based non-profit,non-governmental,humanitarian and relief charity aimed at empowering those in need. we focus on developing 'dharmik sthaans'.

We also promote healty and efficient caring of cows and other animals. we follow and teach the teaching and principles led by Shri shirdi sai baba alongside providing every possible form of assistence for the poor and for deserving children and food for environmental and social improvement.
